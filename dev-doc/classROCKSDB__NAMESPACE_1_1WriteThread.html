◆ JoinBatchGroup()

void ROCKSDB_NAMESPACE::WriteThread::JoinBatchGroup ( Writer *  w)

Wait util: 1) An existing leader pick us as the new leader when it finishes 2) An existing leader pick us as its follewer and 2.1) finishes the memtable writes on our behalf 2.2) Or tell us to finish the memtable writes in pralallel 3) (pipelined write) An existing leader pick us as its follower and finish book-keeping and WAL write for us, enqueue us as pending memtable writer, and 3.1) we become memtable writer group leader, or 3.2) an existing memtable writer group leader tell us to finish memtable writes in parallel.
